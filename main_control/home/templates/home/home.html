ECG Monitoring: The Future of Healthcare


ECG monitoring is a vital tool in modern healthcare, allowing physicians to identify potential cardiac issues in their patients. Our web application takes this to the next level by using sophisticated algorithms to classify live patient data as healthy or unhealthy, and display it for real-time diagnosis. With our easy-to-use interface, healthcare professionals can monitor their patients more efficiently and effectively than ever before.

Overview of ECG Monitoring

ECG Monitoring can be used to detect a range of heart conditions, from arrhythmia to myocardial ischemia. This section explains these conditions and the role that ECG monitoring plays in their diagnosis and treatment.

Arrhythmia

An abnormal heart rhythm that can cause lightheadedness or fainting.

Myocardial Ischemia

A lack of oxygen-rich blood to the heart, which can cause chest pain or a heart attack.

ECG Monitoring

The process of recording the electrical activity of the heart over a period of time.

Diagnosis

The use of medical tools to identify a condition and its severity.
